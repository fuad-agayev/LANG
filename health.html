He took third place at the Paralympic Games - another success from Saley!

Azerbaijani paraswimmer Raman Saley has won another medal at the Paris 2024 Summer Paralympic Games. According to News, the athlete, who competed in the S12 category, took third place in the 100-meter butterfly with a time of 58.13 seconds. Our paraswimmer had previously won a bronze medal in the 100-meter freestyle in the S12 category with a time of 53.65.
